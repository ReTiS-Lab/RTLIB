\subsection{Detailed Description}
This generators produces interarrival times according to a uniform distribution. The distribution is of the form \mbox{[}x(1-\/d), x(1+d)\mbox{]} where x is a random variable between t1 and t2, and d is the percentage of variation. See the constructor for more details.
